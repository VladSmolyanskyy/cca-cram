# CCA Foundations · Master Cram

An interactive, single-page cram web-app for the **Claude Certified Architect (CCA)
Foundations** exam. Every concept from the exam cheat sheet, expanded into diagrams,
runnable code, interactive demos, per-module quizzes, and a flashcard deck.

### ▶︎ Live site

**https://vladsmolyanskyy.github.io/cca-cram/**

Works on desktop and mobile. Your progress, quiz answers, and "got it" ticks are
saved locally in your browser.

### What's inside

- **76 concept cards across 9 modules** — Foundations & the Agentic Loop, Hub-and-Spoke
  Orchestration, Subagents & Sessions, Config & Permissions, Coordination Patterns,
  Resilience, Tool Design & MCP, Prompting & Context, and a Scenario Playbook.
- **9 module checkpoint quizzes** (50 questions) with instant explanations.
- **Flashcard mode** — 225 cards, flip/shuffle/filter by module.
- **Progress tracking** with a sticky progress bar, all persisted in `localStorage`.
